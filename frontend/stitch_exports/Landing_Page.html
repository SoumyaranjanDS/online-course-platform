Frequently Asked Questions

Are the courses self-paced? expand_more
Yes, all courses on Luminous are 100% self-paced. You can start, pause, and resume your learning at any time that fits your schedule.
Do I receive a certificate? expand_more
Every Pro and Enterprise learner receives a digitally verifiable certificate upon successful completion of a course and its final assessment.
Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied? expand_more
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied with the learning experience, just let us know.
Are there group discounts? expand_more
Yes, our Enterprise plan is designed for teams. For groups larger than 20, please contact our sales team for custom pricing.
What kind of hardware do I need? expand_more
As long as you have a modern web browser and a stable internet connection, you can access Luminous from any desktop, tablet, or smartphone.
